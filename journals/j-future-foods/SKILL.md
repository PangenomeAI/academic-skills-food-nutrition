# KeAi / Tsinghua Food Journals — Shared Author Guideline Skill

**Publisher:** KeAi (Elsevier-hosted) / Tsinghua University Press · **Verified:** 2026-07 · Sources: [Journal of Future Foods GfA](https://www.keaipublishing.com/en/journals/journal-of-future-foods/guide-for-authors/), [Food Science and Human Wellness GfA](https://www.keaipublishing.com/en/journals/food-science-and-human-wellness/guide-for-authors/). Both are Gold open access on ScienceDirect; the template follows the Elsevier layout.

## Applies to
| Journal | Abbrev | Scope |
|---|---|---|
| Journal of Future Foods | J FUTURE FOODS | Novel/future foods, sustainable food science |
| Food Science and Human Wellness | FOOD SCI HUM WELL | Food science linked to nutrition, health, wellness |

## Article types
Research articles; Reviews; Short communications; Perspectives.

## Manuscript structure
Title page → Abstract → Keywords → 1. Introduction → 2. Materials and methods → 3. Results and discussion → 4. Conclusions → declarations → References → figure/table legends. Continuous line and page numbering.

- **Abstract:** unstructured, ≤250 words.
- **Keywords:** up to 6.
- **Highlights / graphical abstract:** encouraged (Elsevier-style), confirm per journal.

## Reference style
**Numbered (Vancouver / Elsevier numbered).** In-text `[1]`; numbered reference list with DOIs.

Example:
> [1] A.B. Author, C.D. Author, Title of the article, Journal of Future Foods 3 (2023) 123–130. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jfutfo.2023.xx.xxx

## Figures & tables
Figures ≥300 dpi (line art ≥1000 dpi); vector preferred; ~90 mm / ~190 mm column widths; editable tables; cite in order.

## Submission checklist
- [ ] Abstract ≤250 words · [ ] ≤6 keywords · [ ] Numbered references with DOIs
- [ ] Declaration of competing interest · [ ] Data availability · [ ] CRediT contributions
- [ ] Line + page numbering · [ ] Figures ≥300 dpi · [ ] Cover letter
